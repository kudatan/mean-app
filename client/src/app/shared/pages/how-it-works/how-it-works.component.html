1. The first thing we have to do is to go to the page CATEGORIES There we create our category. It can be, for example, drinks or soups. Add also a photo as desired
2. Then, on the same page, select your category. And add a couple of items to it so that you can place an order
Enter the name and price for your item.
3.After that, go to the page ADD ORDER Select the categories you want
Then select positions and quantities. Then select positions and quantities. Once you have selected all the desired items from the different categories, click COMPLETE
Then you'll see a summary of what you ordered and how much you ordered and how much it totalled.
4.You can look at the order history on the page HISTORY Also after a couple of days of using the app you can see ANALYTICS and OVERVIEW.
